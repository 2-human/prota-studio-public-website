Case study

Exelon EcoCred — F500 innovation incubation, validated.

A multi-year continuous-validation engagement embedded inside the largest US utility’s innovation arm. Consumer mobile app for carbon tracking, sustainable habits, eco-product affiliate sales, and voluntary carbon offsets. Paid acquisition at scale, monetization invalidated, B2B pivot — the canonical operational template for our new Continuous Validation service.

Talk to us about an F500 innovation question

Industry

Utility / sustainability

Engagement

EcoCred consumer carbon-tracking app

Duration

Mid-2019 – Nov 2020 (archive horizon)

Scope

De facto growth + content + monetization + data team

The challenge.

Stand up a consumer mobile product inside a Fortune 100 utility — without the parent’s regulatory perimeter slowing every cycle, without consumer-economics expectations dragging down a brand-authentication play, and with internal funding gates that required Series-A-grade evidence on a defensible cadence.

The solution — Continuous Validation, embedded.

Paid acquisition at scale

Mobile-install campaigns across paid social and Google. Continuous CAC optimization through targeting, creative, and channel-mix tests.

Repeatable experiments framework

Coded experiment templates that scaled across product, growth, monetization, and content surfaces. Mid-month update as methodology — not just a status report.

Series-A diligence checklist

Translated the parent’s internal funding gate into validation acceptance criteria. Kickoff instrument for the entire engagement.

Results.

61,000 installs by November 2020

CAC declined from $3.70 to roughly $1.30 over the engagement; stickiness collapsed 11.5% to 3.7% as paid throttled. iOS 14 ATT broke 51% of high-quality acquisition mid-engagement — attribution mechanics had to be rebuilt on the fly.

Consumer carbon-offset monetization invalidated

60.4% of 2,833 surveyed users would pay $0 for offsets. Total cumulative offset revenue was approximately $2,457 over a year. The validation work definitively closed the consumer monetization path.

B2B pivot recommended

B2B carbon-offset sales for SMBs as brand-authentication; Microsoft’s carbon-fee program as reference architecture. The recommendation reframed the entire strategic ask at the Growth Board.

Post-engagement

Pre-pivot Prota marketing claimed EcoCred subsequently grew to 1M users, was spun out, and sold to an outside firm. The growth and disposition happened after our documented archive horizon; the validation work that shaped the strategic path is what’s directly attributable to the engagement.

Methodology lessons.

EcoCred is the canonical reference for Continuous Validation operating cadence — the operational system our new Continuous Validation service is built on. Specifically:

  • Mid-month update as methodology — recurring deliverable, not a status report.
  • Repeatable experiments framework — coded templates that scale across product / growth / monetization / content surfaces.
  • Series-A diligence checklist as kickoff instrument — translates F500 internal funding gates into validation acceptance criteria.
  • Consumer-vs-utility-economics tension surfaces at Series-A diligence, not engagement start — load-bearing methodology lesson for F500-incubated consumer products.
