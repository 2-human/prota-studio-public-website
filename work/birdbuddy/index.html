Bird Buddy — Smart bird-feeder, co-founded launch.

An AI-powered smart bird feeder co-founded inside the Prota Ventures ecosystem. Industrial design, manufacturer sourcing, supply chain, demand validation, and one of the most successful Kickstarter campaigns to date — funded the seed round and seeded a category.

Talk to us about hardware demand validation

Industry

Smart home / consumer hardware

Engagement

Co-founded launch

Scope

Design + ops + demand validation + launch

Outcome

$5M KS, $11M Y1, 200K units

The challenge.

Design, develop, and launch an AI-powered consumer hardware product into a category — backyard birding — that hadn’t seen a digitally-native entry before. Validate demand at a scale that would justify the manufacturing capex before committing to that capex. Run the launch through a high-profile crowdfunding window where deadline pressure compresses every conversion decision.

The solution — co-founder, not vendor.

Product & operations

Industrial design, prototyping, manufacturer and development-partner sourcing, electrical and mechanical engineering, international supply chain.

Demand validation as the capex gate

Before the production commit, validation work supported the Kickstarter campaign that proved demand at scale. The Kickstarter raise itself funded the seed round.

Co-founder relationship

Prota Ventures became a co-founder in the business — structurally different from the typical Studios services engagement. The line is intentional and set by strategic fit.

Results.

  • $5M raised on Kickstarter — one of the most successful Kickstarter campaigns at the time.
  • $11M in year-one sales.
  • $20M+ in pre-sales within the first year.
  • 200K units shipped in two years.

The Provuu Kickstarter program (highest-funded ski project on Kickstarter, 2025-2026) inherits the playbook from Bird Buddy and the broader Prota Ventures hardware-launch experience. See more work
